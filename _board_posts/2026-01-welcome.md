This is **The Forum** — a place for short, thoughtful pieces from anyone in the community. Unlike our formal journal issues, Forum posts are lighter in tone and length. We review everything before publishing so the Forum stays thoughtful and on-topic.

If you have a reflection, a response to something you read, or an idea you’d like to try out, [submit it]({{ '/submit/' | relative_url }}) and choose “The Forum.” We’d love to hear from you.
