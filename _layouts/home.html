About the Journal

Telos is an informal, self-published undergraduate journal. We write seriously about questions that matter to us — doctrine, history, philosophy, and the life of faith — for readers who share the tradition.

We are not peer-reviewed. We believe the best undergraduate scholarship happens when students write for each other.
